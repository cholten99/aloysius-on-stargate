Iron Mike


I do feel sorry for Dobey, having to deal with S+H on a daily basis - especially when they're giving a very confusing report both at the same time. It's vintage telepathy evidence, with both of them knowing exactly what they're talking about and Dobey not having a clue. The way Hutch rubs it in when Dobey requests that he be the one to give the report rather than Starsky is great, too.

Starsky is eating the entire police station again - and actually steals a chocolate bar from the vending machine (and him an upholder of the law, too!) - but for once Hutch doesn't turn his nose up at Starsky's culinary selections:
Starsky: So where does that leave us, except with us standing here, you eating my lunch?

Cuteness personified later in the same scene:
Hutch: We've got something Ferguson doesn't have.
Starsky: Oh yeah? What's that?
Hutch: Each other.
All I can say is - awwwwwwwww!

S+H undercover at the clothing store is hilarious - I think Starsky rather suits the black negligee, but the blouse Hutch picks up is hideous!

I wonder if they tossed a coin to decide who got to stand on whose head when listening in on Ferguson, or if Hutch just didn't give Starsky a choice - Starsky always seems to draw the short straw in these situations!

Starsky's 'cop on legitimate business' routine when they go to Coyle's office is great - he really throws himself into his roles, doesn't he?

Hutch is wearing his yummy black leather jacket again - it really brings out his eyes. Mmmmmm - oh, sorry - yes, on with the review, got distracted there for a minute...

I just love the chess game at the end. That's the way I play chess, too - be so annoying that the other player is too distracted to be able to play properly. Starsky's definitely got the right idea and he's got irritating Hutch down to a fine art.

I really enjoyed this episode - silliness abounds, but in a good way.
