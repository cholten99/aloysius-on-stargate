The scene in the Torino outside the bar has pretty much all the elements that make S+H who they are rolled into one conversation:
"We're buddies, right? Trust me!"
Hutch being mean - sending Starsky into the bar on his own.
Hutch as the intellectual, having to teach Starsky some Spanish.
Starsky's taste in women being a bone of contention - reference to him setting Hutch up on a bad blind date.
Teasing with obvious underlying affection.

Then of course we get Hutch speeding to the rescue when he thinks Starsky is getting beaten up - that boy can really move when he wants to!

Hutch is his usual touchy-feely self when they get fired upon in the apartment building. He keeps one hand on Starsky until the situation calms down a bit.

S+H beat their silliest undercover act yet again - they do make very convincing mime artists, though. Is there anything these guys can't do?

The tag has Hutch being all grumpy again - jealousy if you ask me (though he's clearly not interested in Laura...). I love the fact that he can predict Starsky's bad jokes, though.
