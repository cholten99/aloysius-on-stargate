Starsky And Hutch Are Guilty


Classic opening conversation between the guys that totally sums up their friendship:
Starsky: Do you trust me or not?
Hutch: With my life? Yes. With your choice of women? No.

I love the way they line up in front of Captain Ryan like naughty schoolboys. Hutch even jiggles Starsky's arm as if he's trying to wind him up - it's very adolescent, but cute all the same.

The premise is a clever idea on the part of the bad guys - it's good to see some imagination from Bay City's criminal masterminds. It does highlight the problem with having such a conspicuous car, though - the lookalikes don't really look that much like our boys, but it's believable that people just see the car and assume it's them.

The 'rousting' of the hookers is pretty funny. I notice Starsky gestures for Hutch to sit on the solitary chair while he plants himself comfortably on the sofa in between the two blondes - though of course it's Hutch who gets offered Nicky's new address, so I guess he gets his own back.

I love the telephone conversation they have when Hutch finds the money Starsky has supposedly dropped off at his apartment:
Starsky: [exasperated because Hutch has interrupted him kissing Sharon] Whaddya want?
Hutch: [all business] Starsk, did you stop by my house about an hour ago?
Starsky: What, are you crazy? I was with you!
Hutch: No, I'm not crazy. Neither are you. And we're both in big trouble.

It's a shame Sharon turns out to be corrupt - she seemed sweet in her first scene, and Starsky seems quite keen on her. I guess I should have known from that she'd either die or be a baddie!

I thought I'd seen some bad clothes in this show, but the outfits S+H wear to the hospital blow all the others right out of the water! Where do they find these clothes? Their act is very funny, though - and worryingly cute! I love the fact that the nurse is completely immune to their cheesy come-on lines, too.

The way they sneak around the PD pretending to clean is hilarious! I imagine they'd probably draw less attention to themselves if they just strolled in wearing their normal clothes, but that would be no fun.

The double Torino chase is brilliant - I love the way the despatcher says, "But that's Starsky's car" when Hutch is trying to explain the situation!

Lots of funny moments, and both guys are very cute when they're confused, so they're pretty cute throughout most of this episode.
