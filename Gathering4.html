Here follow the notes made by Duchess Natalia Romanov at the Gathering of Nations.  These notes were recorded at the time and are here transcribed verbatim - they may be a little confused!
Alexi and I arrived to stormy skies and a confusion of campsites.  There are many more people here than we had expected - ten tribes if the first man we encountered is to be believed.  We have identified the campsite of the Harts but Princess Allegra is not here at present.  The Lady Katerina has welcomed us into her household and we await an audience with the Princess.  There are other guests here - horned creatures from the lands of faerie, a family of the name of Wormwood.

We have been told that a battle has been planned to take place in a few day's time.  It is to be conducted under a Ritual of Peace so as to avoid casualties - apparently, it is supposed to counterfeit a real war, to determine the supremacy of certain tribes over the others.  Alexi seems keen to take part - I shall attempt to conclude our business well before then so that can be avoided.  Meanwhile, we continue to wait, with only a taciturn guard to entertain us.

*

We have at last made contact with her Serene Highness and she seems in good spirits.  She has a companion of the name of Alexander Fairfax.  He is the steward of House Grimear and their relationship is not clear.  Their closeness concerns me.

There are many, may things to make note of.  There are dark fey creatures here, as welcome to wander around as anyone else.  The Princess seems to have made some kind of alliance with the Vipers here - a Lady Dominatrix is attending the pary this evening.  Apparently, the common enemy of the Tartars has brought our Princess together with the Viper faction.  There are also in fact a group of *drow* in league with the Harts!  All things here are very strange and it may take me some time to acclimatise myself.  I have to admit that the informality here is rather alluring.

We have been offered House Grimear colours to wear so that we can be easily identified by the gate guard.

*

Princess Allegra has been made Harts Ambassador to the Tarantulas - the dark fey faction - and I am currently sitting in their bar tent with their High Ambassador, an orc by the name of Og-Nak.  They are a matriarchal society and we have been welcomed by their Matron Mother Kage, a drow queen of some considerable power.  There has been discussion of an insult against the Tarantulas by the Harts that has since been smoothed over, and they are now open to diplomatic relations.  Princess Allegra has asked what she can do for the Tarantulas.  They have a complaint against the Lions, who are hosting this Gathering of Nations, saying that their unliving leaders are declared illegal and thus unable to join in the fight against the Conclace - a common enemy of all factions, bent on destroying the universe.

Og-Nak is speaking about uniting against the Conclave and complaining that the Lions are causing trouble by trying to pick fights with other factions.  It is amazing to me that these dark creatures are proposing reason and peace between the tribes (though they do maintain they are happy to fight whoever comes against them).

Dr Fairfax proposed that the Harts renounce their policy of destroying undead as an example for the other tribes; since the undead are willing to join with us against the Conclave, so we should be willing to accept them as allies.

It seems the alliance between the tribes is extremely fragile and our ambassadorial duties are vitally important in order to keep all parties talking and not resorting to violence.  Tempers are running high, prejudices run deep, and insults so easily develop into feuds.  At the moment, the Tarantulas are holding off from exacting revenge, but it won't be long before things escalate.  I believe it is vital that we develop a more open-minded attitude towards all peoples.  Alexi's suspicions and my own personal fears have no place here - all they will lead to is trouble where there does not need to be any.  The Tarantulas want the Harts to do something about the Lions' policy on the unliving, but without saying they are doing it on behalf of the Tarantulas, as this would be an insult to Og-Nak and would cause a feud with the Harts!

The ambassadors from the Wolf faction have just arrived, showing that at least some of the factions are on friendly relations.

Interestingly, the Tarantula ambassador started the meeting by saying that the way he likes to do business is to speak plainly and to the point so that he doesn't get bored.  And yet he has not said the same thing (about the Lions weakening our combined forces against the Conclave by stopping the unliving from fighting because they will not have back-up) over and over and over again.  I am sure the Princess has taken his point by now!
