Welcome to Aloysius on Stargate! This is basically where I get to express my views on the wonderful show that is Stargate SG-1. Eventually I plan to have reviews for every single episode here, along with any other thoughts and observations I make along the way! Now be warned: not everything I say about the show will be complimentary, but that doesn't mean I don't love it with all my heart. All it means is that I'm a discerning fan, as well as a card-carrying nitpicker!

There are many types of Stargate fans with many different views and opinions. My own personal viewpoint is Daniel-centric, probably focusing more on the friendship between Jack and Daniel than anything else. However, I am proud to be open-minded and eager to hear alternative opinions and interpretations. So, if you read my reviews and want to disagree with me or point out something I've missed -- feel free! You can sign the guestbook (politely, of course) or email me with your viewpoint. And if you just want to tell me that I'm spot on with my observations and that this site is great, that's fine too! :O)

 

That's Aloysius (Al-oo-ish-us) -- teddy bear extraordinaire!
