An Elven Tale
Category: Slash                                                                                                                Rating: PG-13
Notes: I never had any intention of writing LOTR fic but, one day, Legolas just turned up in my head and demanded that I write the film version of events from his point of view.  He proved a rather difficult subject, taking me off in unexpected directions all the time and allowing me no control whatsoever over what I was writing, so I claim none of the credit and take none of the blame for this one!
Also, I should point out that this is based *entirely* on the films and not on the books - anthing that is not explicitly stated in the films I considered fair game, so some rather large liberties have been taken...
